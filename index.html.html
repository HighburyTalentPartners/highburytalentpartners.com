Who We Are

An independent talent partner,
not a recruitment agency

◆ Independent & Freelance Practice

Highbury Talent Partners is an independent consulting practice built to serve a select number of clients. We work directly with investors, founders, and leadership teams across PE and VC-backed healthcare and health tech businesses — providing a highly personalised search and talent advisory service through retained partnerships.

We operate globally with primary focus across the USA and Australia — bringing deep market knowledge and established networks to every engagement. In skill-short, high-competition markets, the best candidates are rarely active. We identify, engage, and secure high-performing talent that traditional recruitment approaches simply don't reach.

Not TransactionalThis is an advisory-led, retained model. We are a long-term partner invested in the outcomes of every search we undertake.
PE & VC FluentWith over five years supporting high-growth, investor-backed businesses, we understand the pressure to scale quickly and deliver returns.
Select Client BaseWe work with a small number of partners at any one time — ensuring every engagement receives the focus and quality it deserves.

How We Work

A retained model
built for outcomes

1
Discovery & Brief

We invest time understanding your business, its investor context, the role's commercial impact, and exactly what success looks like — before we begin.

2
Market Mapping

We map the full landscape of relevant talent — active and passive — drawing on sector networks and proprietary research across the USA, Australia, and global markets.

3
Assessment & Shortlist

Every candidate is rigorously assessed against the brief. You receive a concise, high-quality shortlist — not a high volume of profiles.

4
Placement & Beyond

We manage the process through to offer and onboarding — and remain available as a partner well beyond the placement itself.
